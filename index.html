Knowledge

I am always happy to sare the knowledge I have with others so here is some for you......

The knowledge I am sharing is about my favourite Raga which is khamaj.
'
Khamaj is one of the ten Thaats (parent scales) of Hindustani music. It is also a specific raga within the

Aaroh (asscending order of swaras)- S G M P D N S

Avroh (desscending order of swaras)- S N D P M G R S The Nishad is komal in the avroh.

Pakad (The part in a raga which helps you recognize it without anyone telling you it's name)-N D, M P D, M G The Nishad is also Komal in the Pakad

All ragas have more than 2 different Bandish and Swar Malika. I have shared 1 bandish and 1 Swar malika. Each swar malika is divided into 2 parts- Sthai and Antra. Sthai comes first and Antra comes second.
